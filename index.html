Online Farming:

  • Focus on farming Wing Essence and Synthetic Stones.
  • Farm the highest stage where you can consistently achieve 1500 kills per minute.

Offline Farming:

  • Not recommended, only when you can't online farm.
  • Worth doing a few hours after a big damage upgrade for traits.

Altar Progress

  • Prioritize leveling one equipment (ATK/HP) to level 25.
  • Every 5 levels of the altar yield a substantial damage boost.

Awakening Dungeon - Skill Rotation

  • Enhanced/Buff Build
    • Start with dagger, Auto Switch Off, Auto Attack On
    • Enter Dungeon
    • Wait for the last dagger skill to activate, Auto Switch On to bow
  • Active Build
    • Start with bow, Auto Switch Off, Auto Attack On
    • Enter Dungeon
    • Wait for the last bow skill to activate, Auto Switch On to dagger
