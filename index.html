Online Farming

  • Focus on farming Wing Essence and Synthetic Stones.
  • Farm the highest stage where you can consistently achieve 1500 kills per minute.

Offline Farming

  • Not recommended, only when you can't online farm.
  • Worth doing a few hours after a big damage upgrade for traits.

Altar Progress

  • Prioritize leveling one equipment from ATK and HP to level 25. I chose Dagger And Shoe.
  • Every 5 levels of the altar yield a substantial damage boost so leveling equally will slow you down.

Guild Core Progress

  • Easy way: Your minor runes should be twice the level of your core rune(2:1).
  • Efficient way: Follow the rule of (3,6,9,-20,3).
  • Aim for this ratio. Your stats should look like this:
      • (79 Speed, 82 HP, 85 Attack, 59 Normal, 79 Boss, Core ~40)
  • Core level should around half of your attack - a few level at most.
    • At level 79 attack, the math points to an optimal level 36 core rune with the guild package.
    • Without guild package the cost of core runes are expensive.
    • This creates a different optimal core rune level, a few lower, without the guild package

Awakening Dungeon - Skill Rotation

  • Enhanced/Buff Build
    • Start with dagger, Auto Switch Off, Auto Attack On
    • Enter Dungeon
    • Wait for the last dagger skill to activate, Auto Switch On to bow
  • Active Build
    • Start with bow, Auto Switch Off, Auto Attack On
    • Enter Dungeon
    • Wait for the last bow skill to activate, Auto Switch On to dagger
