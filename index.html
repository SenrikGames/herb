Enhanced vs Buff vs Active

    Active

  • Top damage

    • Buff

  • Ideal for online farming near max level. Slightly slower without enhanced damage.

    • Enhanced

  • Best for early to mid-game.
  • Combines with both active/buff build for reaching higher kills per minute.

Online Farming

  • Aim for Wing Essence and Synthetic Stones.
  • Farm at stages where you can get 1500 kills per minute.

Offline Farming

  • Not recommended, only when you can't online farm.
  • Worth doing a few hours after a big damage upgrade for traits.

Altar Progress

  • Level all altars equally until level 10.
  • Then focus on one equipment from Attack and HP.
  • Aim for level 15 in one equipment first.
  • The second equipment will take less time to reach level 15
  • Every 5 levels of the altar yield a substantial damage boost.

2 Star Red Relic

  • Best acquired after two altar equipments reach level 15.

    • Guild Core Progress

    • Easy Method: Keep minor runes at double the level of your core rune(2:1).
    • Efficient Method: Follow the (3,6,9,-20,3) rule.
    • Aim for this ratio. Your stats should look like:
        • Speed 79, HP 82, Attack 85, Normal 59, Boss 79, Core around 40.
    • Core level should around half of your attack - a few level at most.
      • At level 79 attack, the math points to an optimal level 36 core rune with the guild package.
      • Without guild package the cost of core runes are expensive.
      • This creates a different optimal core rune level, a few lower, without the guild package
